The Simpsons

The Simpsons is a long-running American animated sitcom that follows the everyday life of the Simpson family in the town of Springfield. It mixes comedy, satire, and social commentary, and has become one of the most influential TV shows in history. The series is known for its humor, iconic characters, and its ability to stay relevant across generations.

About

  • Created By: Matt Groening
  • Country: United States
  • Nr of Seasons: 36 Seasons
  • Nr of Episodes: 780+ Episodes
  • Animation Studio: Gracie Films & Film Roman
  • Production Company: 20th Television Animation

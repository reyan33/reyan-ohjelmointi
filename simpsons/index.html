The Simpsons

About the Series

The Simpsons is an American animated television series created by Matt Groening. The show follows the Simpson family living in the fictional town of Springfield.

The main characters are Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa and Maggie Simpson. The series first aired in 1989 and has become one of the most famous animated television shows in the world.

Moe's Tavern

Moe's Tavern is Springfield's iconic dive bar where Homer and his friends gather for cheap beer, awkward conversations, and the kind of trouble only Moe Szyslak can attract.

The gallery images show Moe behind the bar, a noisy night with patrons, the famous regulars, the front entrance with a waiting line, a neon beer sign, the pool table area, and the tavern exterior.
